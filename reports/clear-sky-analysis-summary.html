🧮 Score Calculation Breakdown

Formula: ((100-cloudCover)/100 * 70 + transparency/5 * 15 + seeing/5 * 15) / 100

Example for Hour 0:

  • Cloud contribution: (100-5)/100 × 70 = 66.5
  • Transparency contribution: 4/5 × 15 = 12
  • Seeing contribution: 3/5 × 15 = 9
  • Total Score: (66.5 + 12 + 9) = 87.5 → 88/100
